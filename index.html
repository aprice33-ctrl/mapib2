How this model works

This is an engineered decision model, not unreliable live scraping. It translates geopolitical news themes into household pressure. International and U.S. news coverage often frames the same issues differently: Reuters and AP emphasize markets and verified developments; BBC and El País emphasize global diplomatic context; The New York Times and The Washington Post emphasize policy and institutions; Fox News and MSNBC frame risks through competing domestic political priorities.

Perspective basket: Reuters, Associated Press, BBC News, El País, The New York Times, The Washington Post, Fox News, MSNBC, El Nacional, El Universal, Efecto Cocuyo, El Pitazo, teleSUR, Agencia Venezolana de Noticias, Diario VEA, and Venezuelanalysis.
